What we do

Services for every stage

Selling your property

Valuation and assessment of your home, professional photos and video, and effective marketing to reach our network of investors and the main listing platforms.

Renting out your home

We find the tenant who best fits your conditions, reducing the risk of unpaid rent, and draft contracts with full guarantees.

Advice and negotiation

We support you from the first consultation to closing: skilled negotiation, renovation advice, and coordination with notaries and insurers.

Paperwork management

We handle the paperwork for you: contracts, key custody, furniture removal and cleaning, so all you need to worry about is moving in.

Energy efficiency certificates

We arrange your energy performance certificate (EPC), mandatory for selling or renting, along with advice on optimising the home's energy use.

Multilingual support

We assist you in Spanish, English and French, so you get the same close attention whatever your language.
